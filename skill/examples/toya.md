# Example — TOYA (durable discount + buyback catalyst)

**One-line lesson:** a company that is "always cheap" for a structural/sentiment
reason can be a repeatable idea *if* you re-derive forward C/Z vs implied fair
cap each cycle, and treat a management buyback as an **active catalyst**
underwriting the gap — not just a nice-to-have.

## How Malik reasoned it (2023–2026, recurring)

TOYA priced as if a construction/consumer slowdown were permanent, when he
judged further deterioration unlikely — *"spółka wiecznie niedowartościowana."*
Catalyst chain: (1) cyclical recovery in construction/consumer, (2) falling
China freight + a new warehouse improving efficiency, (3) an expected **share
buyback / skup** once inventory cash was freed — the buyback itself a second,
market-structure catalyst that reduces float and supports the price.

Numbers as cited (2026): own profit forecast ~**95 mln zł**; at forward C/Z
**10** → implied fair cap ≈ **950 mln zł** vs market cap **733 mln zł** (recently
< 650) — a ~20–30% discount. Outcome: **multi-year, repeatedly re-confirmed**
discount; buy near lows, benefit from tender premium / re-rating each cycle.

## Verdict shape this should produce

- **thesis:** "Trwały dyskont do wartości godziwej (forward C/Z 10 ⇒ ~950 mln zł
  vs ~733 mln zł kap.); katalizatory: odbicie cykliczne, spadek frachtu, oraz
  skup akcji wspierający kurs."
- **catalysts:** `{type: cyclical, priced_in: partial}`,
  `{type: capital-structure (skup akcji), horizon: 1–2 kwartały,
  priced_in: false}`
- **checklist highlights:** valuation vs own history — *spełnia*; balance sheet /
  cash generation — *spełnia*; catalyst — *spełnia* (buyback), though partly
  recurring; size — mid, note it.
- **red_flags / self-check:** "czy to analiza, czy przywiązanie do spółki?" —
  Malik explicitly asks this of durable-discount names (the Decora self-check).
- **alignment_score:** solid (70s) — capped short of the 80s because the
  catalyst is partly a recurring cyclical rather than a fresh, one-time repricing
  trigger.
- **verify_next:** "Potwierdzenie skupu i jego skali; sygnały odbicia w
  budownictwie/konsumpcji; czy prognoza 95 mln zł się utrzymuje."

**Teaching point:** a discount alone is a value trap; the buyback is what turns
"wiecznie niedowartościowana" into an actionable, catalysed thesis.
